This host serves the firmware releases for Water Polo Board hardware for over-the-air (OTA) installations from our [Water Polo Suite](https://lgias.com/) apps.

You do not need to download anything here - the apps will.

If you are setting up your Water Polo Board for the first time, start at [lgias.com/flash](https://lgias.com/flash).

The firmware is provided as is. See the [Terms of Use](https://lgias.com/terms).
